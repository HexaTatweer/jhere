I am a software engineer, web dev and JavaScript geek. I work with many other clever people on HERE, in Berlin. Coding is my job as well as my favourite hobby. You can find almost everything I work on by looking at my Github profile or my blog.

If you want to leave some feedback about jHERE, feel free to just email me at max at jhere dot net. Any feedback is good, really. And if you want to contribute, just fork the project and submit a pull request.

Note that even though I work for Nokia on HERE, jHERE is a project that I developed in my personal time and it is not related to or supported by Nokia or HERE in any way.
